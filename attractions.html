Attractions

Places to Visit

Best tourist destinations & places in Dhanaulti

Surkanda Devi Temple

Surkanda Devi is a Hindu temple near our camp. It is at an altitude of about 2756 metres lies close to nearby hill stations of Dhanaulti which is approx 6 KM from Our Camp & Cottage.

Tapovan Trek

Tapovan is a naturally diverse area carpeted by the lush green grasslands and beautiful rocks.

Eco Park

Eco Park in Dhanaulti is developed  by the Divisional Forest Officer (DFO) and citizens of Dhanaulti, which houses the deodar trees. Eco Park is an amazing place for the adventurous tourists. 

Himalayan View

Location of our Camp is perfect for Himalayan View. You will enjoy the morning tea with mountains.

Adventure Activities

“Flying Like a Bird” in the beautiful mountains. Dhanaulti is the ideal place for those who want to experiences the full of adventure activities.

Snowfall

A popular destination to see snowfall in India for mountain lovers, this place is covered with a beautiful sheet of snow in the peak winters.

Aloo Khet

Aaloo Khet is a potato farm that belongs to the government. The production of potatoes is substantial and is supplied to many places outside the state of Uttarakhand.

Deodar Trees

Spread over an area of hectares covered in deodar and oak trees. It is a truly magnificent sight to feel the calmness and enjoy nature.
Its a must visit place for nature lovers and people who like peace.

Mussoorie

Mussoorie is one of the most popular hill stations located with a backdrop of the Shivalik range of Himalayas and doon valley, Mussoorie, also known as Queen of The Hills, stands at an altitude of approx. 7000 feet above sea level.
