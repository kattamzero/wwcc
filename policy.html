Booking can be confirmed by paying 50% of the total package cost and by providing Govt. ID proof of primary Guest. Rest of the Amount has to paid at Check-in. Camps will be allotted only after full(remaining) payment at check-in.

1. Before 7 days of the Check-in date-time: 100% refund OR else NO REFUND
2. Guaranteed reservation will be held until 1800 Hours. Unless a late arrival is specified.
3. Early check-in (before 8:00am) will be charged Rs. 1000 extra per Camp & Cottage subject to availability of
camp. If the camp is not available, Guest may enjoy mountain views from free area.
4. Cancellations must be processed via the same system of booking. 

Children below the age of 5 Years sharing parent’s bed shall not be charged for accommodation. However, if extra bed is required extra bed rate will be applicable. 

As per Government regulations, we request all guests (on single/double/triple occupancy) to carry a photo identity to present on check-in. Foreign nationals are required to present their valid passport and visa.
Indian nationals can present any of the mentioned government-issued photo identity address proof card. Aadhar-Card (most preferred), Driving License, Passport or Voter’s ID card.

Note: PAN Card will not be accepted.

Refund shall be initiated within 48 hours of receiving the request and the payment would be credited within 5-7 working days.

Guest are requested to observe, abide by confirm to and be bound by all applicable acts, Laws, Government rules and regulations in force from time to time.
