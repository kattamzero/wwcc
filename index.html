It's time to start your adventures

This magical hill station is perched at an elevation of 2286 meters above sea level and offers panoramic views of the lofty Himalayas. Dhanaulti is a place for those who are seeking a holiday of privacy in the hills away from the hustle-bustle of city life. Dhanaulti is a paradise for the tourist and it's pristine beauty will rejuvenate your mind, body and soul.
